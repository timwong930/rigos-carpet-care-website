# Outreach

## Initial contact
- **Opener:** Hi, I’m Tim — I took a quick look at Rigo's Carpet Care & Services and put together a simple idea for how the website could turn more visitors into calls or quote requests.
- **What to mention:**
  - Lead with trust: before/after photos, easy call/text buttons, and clearer service blocks.
  - Mention that cleaning customers want proof, speed, and a simple quote path.
- **Ask:** Offer a quick screenshot of how the homepage could convert better on mobile.
- **Tone:** Friendly, local, no-pressure, and short. Keep it practical and specific.
