# Rigo's Carpet Care & Services

Starter website draft for **Rigo's Carpet Care & Services** based on public information from the company website.

## Business summary
- Residential and commercial carpet cleaning
- 24/7 water damage extraction
- Eco-friendly / green cleaning
- Licensed and bonded
- Evenings and weekends available
- Santa Ana, CA / Orange County

## Why this looks like a good opportunity
The business already has real trust signals and a clear local service offering, but the current site feels busy and template-driven. A cleaner, more modern website could make the business look more premium and improve conversions.

## Suggested redesign angle
- Make the hero more focused
- Put the phone number and booking CTA front and center
- Reduce slideshow clutter
- Use fewer, stronger images
- Turn trust signals into a clean badge row
- Keep the review proof and emergency service messaging obvious

## Lead summary for outreach
This business looks like a strong candidate for a simple redesign that makes the site feel faster, cleaner, and easier to trust.
